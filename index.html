Personnel Records Review

Personnel Record Review is to ensure that data on the Record Brief is correct and that all source documents required for filing in the Army Military Human Resource Record (AMHRR) are in iPERMS
Go to Website

Records Review Requirements

  • How to Guide (https://www.hrc.army.mil/asset/14840)
  • Key Supporting Documents Matrix (KSD) (https://www.hrc.army.mil/content/16487)
  • The Soldier’s latest end of month LES, Unit Commanders Finance Report, or Unit Commanders Pay Management Report

Methods of Executing the Review

There are three methods of executing the review:
  1. Soldier Present (Joint) –Soldier and Record Manager at same workstation (dual CAC readers –most preferred)
  2. Independent–Soldier and Record Manager at different workstations (acceptable, but not most preferred)
  3. Soldier Unavailable –No Soldier involvement due to extenuating circumstances, such as confinement, hospitalization, AWOL (least preferred)

Servicemembers’ Group Life Insurance Online Enrollment System (SOES)

SOES is the Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance (SGLI) On-Line Enrollment System. It is an enterprise system for the Uniformed Services and provides an authoritative source for SGLI and FSGLI information. It replaces the paper-based SGLI/FSGLI enrollment, maintains elections and beneficiary information, and provides 24/7 self-service access to Service members. SOES centralizes SGLI/FSGLI data into one authoritative system capable of providing consistent SGLI/FSGLI information to members and their leadership.

What can the Soldiers do in the SOES Application?

  • Increase, reduce or cancel SGLI and FSGLI coverage
  • Add a beneficiary or edit SGLI beneficiary information
  • Complete a Medical Questionnaire to increase their coverage
  • View, save, print or email a SGLI Coverage Certificate
